How to reach IGDTUW :

Arrival by AIR:
If you are coming to Delhi by air, we strongly recommended to fly into Delhi Airport. Delhi airport is located about 80 kms from IGDTUW. You can hire taxis at the airport.It takes anywhere between two and three hours to drive from the Delhi airport to the IGDTUW.

Arrival by TRAIN:
New Delhi Railway station is well connected to most cities in North, East and South India. IGDTUW is located at a distance of about 16 kilometers from the New Delhi Railway Station.

Arrival by METRO:
The nearest metro station is Kashmere Gate only and exit from Gate No.7 has a walking distance of abut 5-7 mins to the college.
